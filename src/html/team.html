Our Team

Meet the amazing group of people that make SIPSTACK possible.

We are a team of bold thinkers and enthusiastic minds. We want to build the most future proof solutions to the most relevant communication challenges facing the world today. We are masters of balance. We celebrate individuality and individual success; we also work amazingly well together as a team.

We cherish brilliance, independent thinking and creativity.

Jonathan Geller

Chief Executive Officer

Jonathan has been disassembling and wiring back electronics since he was five years old. His life is full of genius founder stories: hack into high school computer network, start computer company in high school, drop out of college to start a business. He's founded three companies since, including Telair in 2005. SIPSTACK is how he's going to revolutionize VoIP.

Stam Loizides

Chief Innovation Officer

Stam is the secret ingredient that ties the team together. It's rumoured that he has an MBA, and that he's lived and worked in ten different countries. No one really knows what he does (including himself). What we do know is that he can tackle every problem from infinite perspectives, which makes everything tremendously better.

Errol Geller

Chief Financial Officer

Errol loves taking on challenges and is very good at math. He has several decades of experience as a Senior Risk Management Executive at IBM. Errol is instrumental in getting SIPSTACK to where it is today. 

Kanasu Nagathihalli
